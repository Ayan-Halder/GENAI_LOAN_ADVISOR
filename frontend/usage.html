GenAI Loan Advisor - Usage Instructions

This section provides an overview of how to use the GenAI Loan Advisor application. Follow these steps to interact with the tool:

Step 1: Access the Web Application

Open your web browser and go to:

http://127.0.0.1:8000/

Step 2: Fill Out the Form

In the web interface, you will see a form with several fields. Enter the following details:

  • Loan Type: Choose the type of loan (e.g., Home Loan, Car Loan).
  • Credit Score: Enter your credit score (e.g., 700).
  • Loan Term: Specify the duration of the loan in years (e.g., 15).
  • Annual Income: Enter your annual income in your chosen currency.
  • Loan Amount: Enter the amount you wish to request for the loan.

Step 3: Submit the Form

Click on the Submit button to send your details. The backend will process the input and display the loan advice on the same page.

Step 4: Review the Results

The results will include suggestions based on your input. Use these insights to make an informed decision about your loan request.

Tip

If you want to try different inputs, modify the values in the form and click Submit again. The new results will replace the previous ones.
